  • University of Sydney Transfer

    sydney does coffee differently than in the US and it’s
    defintely something to get used to. we were there during
    summer time so naturally wanted our drinks to be iced. whenever
    we would order an iced coffee it would come with coffee,
    sugar/sweetening up, ice cream, ice and whipped cream. if you
    like dessert for breakfast then you’re in the clear but otherwise
    you’re better off ordering a hot drink or making your own iced drinks.
    - Kate Christie '21

  • University of Copenhagen Engineering Exchange

    The Copenhagen Engineering Exchange program is
    actually located in Lyngby, Denmark! The location is
    misadvertised, but Denmark itself is so small and flat
    that biking between the school and city is common for students.
    A decent bike will run you back about 1000DK, but it is a
    worthwhile investment for the exchange and you can usually sell it
    to an incoming student. For all you short people out there, you might
    be able to get away with children's bikes as the Danes are very tall!
    - Zoe Yu '19
